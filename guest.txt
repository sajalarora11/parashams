A guest lectures was conducted by the department of Information Technology with Mr. Rajat Kumar from HP, Singapore, who joined us through vidop Conference on Friday 21 July to tell us about a very interesting and important topic, Data Science. 
In that guest lectures, we were told about the data driven science i.e. Data Science. We learnt that Data Science is an interdisciplinary field about scientific methods, processes, and systems to extract knowledge or insights from data in various forms, either structured or unstructured, similar to data mining. He also shared the importance and uses of various topics such as Word Cloud, Data Engineering, Tableau with certain examples etc. Then finally, there was a great session for the intraction of him with the students. It was quite a quality time spending in that guest lecture.
